Category 01 · The most important

God ✝️

Literally the most important thing in my life. My sole purpose on this earth is to glorify my Father.

I do not play with my Heavenly Father. You see, I am a sinner, and I deserve death — because the wages of sin is death, blame Adam — but do I get death? No! Because God loved us so amazingly that He gave His own Son. Let me rephrase: He told His own Son, Jesus, to come and take our punishment so that we do not die.

All we have to do is believe that Jesus is Lord, that He died for us, and have faith. That is it. That is the whole thing.
